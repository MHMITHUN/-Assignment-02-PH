Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Our kindergarten program focuses on early childhood development through play-based learning, creative activities, and social interaction. We help build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and emotional intelligence.

Elementary School

Our elementary program provides comprehensive education in core subjects while fostering critical thinking and creativity. Students engage in hands-on learning experiences and develop essential academic and life skills.

Middle (10-16) Years

Our middle school curriculum challenges students with advanced concepts while supporting their emotional and social growth. We prepare them for higher education through project-based learning and academic excellence.
